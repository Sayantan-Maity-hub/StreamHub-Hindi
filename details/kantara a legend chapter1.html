Storyline

“Kantara: A Legend – Chapter 1” is a prequel to Kantara (2022), exploring the origins of the divine legend that shaped the events of the first film. Set in the ancient Kadamba dynasty era, it delves into the mystical bond between humans, nature, and the divine spirit Panjurli Daiva — a protector deity who safeguards the forest and its people. The story follows the birth of a legend, tracing how divine powers first came to the land and how the balance between man and nature was formed.
